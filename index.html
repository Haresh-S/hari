Tribute to AB de Villiers

AB de Villiers, also known as Mr. 360, is a legendary cricketer known for his incredible versatility and unmatched skills on the field. His ability to adapt to any situation and play shots all around the ground has made him a fan favorite worldwide.

Born on February 17, 1984, in Pretoria, South Africa, AB de Villiers made his international debut in 2004 and quickly rose to prominence with his exceptional batting performances.

Throughout his career, de Villiers achieved numerous milestones and played a pivotal role in many of South Africa's memorable victories. He holds multiple records and is considered one of the greatest cricketers of all time.
